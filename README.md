# Lead-Quality-Analysis-Case-Study

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing lead quality trends and identifying the factors influencing high-quality lead generation for an advertiser.

The dataset contains approximately 3,000 leads along with information related to:

Lead status
Ad creatives (Widgets)
Traffic partners
Debt levels
Address & phone verification scores
Campaign & referral information

The objective of this analysis was to identify:

  -Whether lead quality improved or declined over time
  -The key drivers influencing lead quality
  -Opportunities to improve lead quality and increase CPL profitability

#KPIs Used : 
  Total Leads = Count of VendorLeadIDs
  Good Leads = Leads with statuses:
  Lead Quality % = Good Leads / Total Leads

#Tools Used :
Excel,
Power BI,
Data Cleaning,
KPI Analysis,
Business & Segment Analysis

#Analysis Performed :
1. Lead Quality Trend Analysis
-Analyzed lead quality trends over a 6-month period
-Observed fluctuations rather than a consistent upward/downward trend
-Identified the need to investigate underlying drivers affecting lead quality
2. Segment & Driver Analysis
-AddressScore & PhoneScore
-Higher PhoneScore segments showed stronger and more stable lead quality
-Higher AddressScore segments delivered more scalable and reliable performance
3. Widget / Ad Creative Analysis
-Certain widgets significantly outperformed others (~24% lead quality)
-Demonstrated the impact of ad creatives on conversion quality
4. Partner Performance
-Lead quality varied significantly across partners
-Some partners delivered higher-quality traffic than others
5. Debt Level Analysis
-Mid-to-high debt segments performed better
-Extremely high debt segments underperformed

#Key Business Recommendations
-Scale high-performing ad creatives and traffic sources
-Prioritize leads with stronger verification scores
-Reduce dependency on low-quality traffic channels
-Focus more on profitable debt-level segments

#Outcome

The analysis provided actionable insights to improve overall lead quality, optimize acquisition strategy, and support higher CPL negotiations with the advertiser.
